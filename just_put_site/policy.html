Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Just Put. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app. By using Just Put, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Just Put does not collect any personal information from our users. All data entered into the app is saved locally on the user's device and is not accessible to us.

Permissions

Just Put requires access to your camera and storage in order to function properly. The camera is used to scan QR codes and create the photo, while storage access is required to save your images and your projects. We do not collect or store any of this information ourselves.

Ads

Just Put displays Google ads in the app. These ads are subject to Google's own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We do not collect any data related to the ads displayed in Just Put.

Contact Information

Children’s Privacy

Childrens under the age of 13 can use this app. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian, you don't need to worry as all your child's data is stored locally on the device and is not sent to us in any way.
